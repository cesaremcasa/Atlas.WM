# Atlas.WM v3.0 Roadmap

**Start Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Target Completion:** March 1, 2026 (2 weeks)  
**Status:** Planning Phase  
**Previous:** v2.0 (Architecture validated, environment insufficient)

## Executive Summary

v3.0 will add sufficient environment complexity to demonstrate learning vs memorization. The v2.0 architecture is production-ready and will be preserved.

**Core Change:** 3 objects to 10+ objects with partial observability.

## Why v3.0 is Necessary

v2.0 showed architecture works but environment was too simple.

v3.0 will demonstrate real generalization with visible train/val gap.

## Design Philosophy

### Keep What Works
- Structured encoder
- Hard static constraint
- Training infrastructure

### Upgrade What Failed
- 10 objects (vs 3)
- Partial observability
- Variable masses
- Process noise

## Technical Specification

### Environment: CruelGridworld v7

- grid_size: 20
- num_objects: 10
- observation_radius: 8.0
- partial observability

## Complexity Sources

1. **Combinatorial Explosion:** 45 pairwise interactions
2. **Partial Observability:** Hidden state inference required
3. **Variable Physics:** Random masses, friction zones
4. **Process Noise:** Stochastic outcomes

## Expected Training
```
Epoch 1  | Train: 0.35 | Val: 0.42
Epoch 30 | Train: 0.06 | Val: 0.09
Epoch 50 | Train: 0.03 | Val: 0.05
```

Train/val gap indicates real generalization.
